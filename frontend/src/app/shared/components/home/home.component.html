Welcome to the Alcalá Record Books. The Alcalá Record Books are a digitisation of an 18th century books of accounts from the Royal Irish College in Alcalá de Henares, Spain. Established in 1649, the college served as training for Catholic Priests from Ireland (Catholicism being outlawed in Ireland at the time). It existed until 1785 when it was absorbed into the Royal Irish College in Salamanca, Spain.

The records here specifically deal with the monetary accounts as recorded by the rector of the college from 1774 to 1781 (excluding 1780 which is missing from the original manuscript). The original manuscript is currently on display in the Russell Library at Maynooth University in Maynooth, Co. Kildare, Ireland.
